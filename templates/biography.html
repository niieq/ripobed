BIOGRAPHY

Obed Kwame Ademang was born on the 12th of January, 1991 in Somanya in the Eastern Region to Mr. Joseph Amanor Ademang and Mrs. Sarah Ademang.

Obed Kwame Ademang accepted Jesus Christ at an early age thus a staunch Catolic until his demise.

In 2009, Obed Kwame Ademang started his secondary education in Aggrey Memorial A.M.E. Zion senior high School. While in High School, Obed studied Business. In this time, Obed matured and took up leadership roles, as he was the President of the Catholic Students Union(2008/2009). He doubled as a member of the Dining Hall Committee and also served in the school choir. Not withstanding his leadership roles, Obed was a model student and excelling academically. In 2011, Obed furthered his education to the University Of Cape Coast were he studied BSc. Information Technology. He was the president of the Computer Science and Information Technology Students Association(2014/2015) and also the team lead at Google Developers Group(2014/2015). He was instrumental in the revamping of the Computer Science and Information Technology Students Association. With all these responsibilities, Obed was able to complete UCC with Honors in 2015.

Upon Completing University Of Cape Coast, Obed worked with the department as a Teaching Assistant from the year 2015 to 2016. Obed had an entrepreneural spirit which lead him to venture into startups with his likeminded friends as a side business. In 2017 he worked with Ahonya Dot Com as a Software Engineer. Obed finally landed a job as a Software Developer at the University Of Cape Coast where he worked until his demise.

Obed Kwame Ademang departed this life on the 11th of May 2020, preceding a short illness which he was reported to be recovering from. Obed was a cherished son, a brother and friend to many but we take solace that he is resting well in the Lord's Bosom.
